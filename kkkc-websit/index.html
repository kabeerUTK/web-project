KHUSAL KHAN KHATTAK DEGREE COLLEGE

Khushal Khan Khattak Degree College is a reputable institution dedicated to academic excellence and holistic student development.
Named after the legendary Pashto poet and warrior, the college upholds a legacy of cultural pride and intellectual pursuit.
With a committed faculty and modern facilities, it offers quality education across various disciplines.
The college fosters critical thinking, discipline, and leadership among its students.

Courses We Offer

BS Computer Science

The BS Computer Science program at GDCKKK focuses on programming, data structures, and emerging technologies. It equips students with the skills needed for software development, research, and IT industry careers.

BS Economics

The BS Economics program provides a strong foundation in microeconomics, macroeconomics, and data analysis. Students gain analytical and decision-making skills essential for roles in finance, policy, and research.

At the intermediate level, the college provides Arts and Science courses including Biology and Computer Science. These courses prepare students for higher education and develop core subject knowledge.
